What are Pedestal Columns?

Pedestal columns are short, reinforced concrete structures that provide additional support to heavy loads. They help in transferring loads efficiently to the foundation and enhance the overall stability of buildings and industrial structures.

Purpose of Pedestal Columns:
  • Distributes Load Efficiently: Transfers heavy loads to the foundation.
  • Prevents Structural Failure: Reduces stress on the main columns.
  • Enhances Stability: Provides extra reinforcement in load-bearing areas.
  • Supports Machinery & Heavy Structures: Used in industrial applications.
