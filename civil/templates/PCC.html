What is Plain Cement Concrete (PCC)?

Plain Cement Concrete (PCC) is a mixture of cement, sand, aggregate, and water, used to provide a smooth and rigid base for foundations. It acts as a protective layer and distributes loads evenly to prevent settlement and cracks.

Purpose of PCC:
  • Protects Reinforcement: Prevents steel from corrosion.
  • Provides a Strong Base: Offers a stable surface for structures.
  • Distributes Load Evenly: Minimizes settlement and structural cracks.
  • Improves Durability: Enhances the lifespan of the construction.
