What is Site Cleaning?

Site cleaning is the process of preparing land for construction by removing unwanted materials, debris, and vegetation. It ensures a safe and efficient working environment before excavation and foundation work begins.

Steps in Site Cleaning:
  • Clearing Vegetation: Removing trees, bushes, and plants.
  • Debris Removal: Cleaning waste materials and rubble.
  • Surface Leveling: Flattening uneven ground.
  • Soil Testing & Preparation: Ensuring the land is ready for construction.
