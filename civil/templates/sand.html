What is Sand Filling?

Sand filling is a construction technique where sand is layered and compacted to provide a strong foundation. It helps in improving soil stability, reducing settlement, and preventing soil erosion.

Importance of Sand Filling:
  • Prevents Soil Erosion: Provides better drainage and stability.
  • Enhances Load-Bearing Capacity: Strengthens the foundation.
  • Reduces Settlement Issues: Helps in compacting loose soil.
  • Provides a Stable Base: Essential for foundation and flooring work.
