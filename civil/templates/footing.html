What is Footing Concrete?

Footing concrete is a structural base used to distribute a building’s weight evenly over the ground, preventing settlement and ensuring stability. It is a crucial component in foundation construction.

Types of Footings:
  • Isolated Footing: Supports individual columns.
  • Combined Footing: Supports multiple columns.
  • Strip Footing: Supports continuous walls.
  • Raft (Mat) Footing: Covers the entire building area.
