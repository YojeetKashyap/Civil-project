What is Excavation of Site?

Excavation is the process of removing earth, soil, and rock to create space for construction foundations, pipelines, and landscaping. It is an essential step in site preparation.

Types of Excavation:
  • Topsoil Excavation: Removal of surface soil.
  • Earth Excavation: Digging below topsoil for foundations.
  • Rock Excavation: Removing rock layers.
  • Muck Excavation: Clearing wet, muddy soil.
